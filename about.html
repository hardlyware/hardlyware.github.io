About HARDlyWARE

HARDlyWARE explores the space between ideas and physical hardware — electronics, 3D prints, robots, and experimental builds that are meant to be taken apart, improved, and learned from.

The goal is to document the full journey: from first sketches and breadboards to custom PCBs, enclosures, and working prototypes. Each project aims to be transparent about design decisions, trade-offs, and the messy bits in between.

HARDlyWARE exists to share practical, reproducible builds and to encourage more people to design, debug, and ship their own hardware.
